# Food & Beverage Pack

> 🚧 **In development** — contributions welcome. See [Build Your Own Pack](/industries/custom).

## Planned Constraints

- `food.allergen` — no cross-contamination between allergen-containing and allergen-free products
- `food.fefo` — First Expired, First Out — use ingredients with earliest expiry first
- `food.temperature-zone` — chilled, ambient, and frozen production cannot share the same room
- `food.cleaning-regime` — wet vs. dry cleaning by product category
- `food.best-before` — finished product best-before date must meet customer minimum shelf life
