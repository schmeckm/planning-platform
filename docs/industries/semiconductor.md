## Planned Constraints

- `semi.reticle-availability` — photolithography reticle must be available and qualified for the product layer
- `semi.wet-bench-chemistry` — chemistry changeover sequences in wet etch stations
- `semi.equipment-qualification` — equipment must be qualified for the specific product and layer
- `semi.hot-lot-priority` — hot lots override standard FIFO sequencing
